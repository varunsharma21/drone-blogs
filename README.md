How do delivery drones work?

Delivery drones operate through a sophisticated integration of technology, including GPS navigation, sensors, and advanced software systems. These unmanned aerial vehicles are designed to transport packages efficiently and quickly, often bypassing traditional road traffic. The drones are equipped with GPS systems that allow them to determine their precise location and navigate to their destination with remarkable accuracy. Additionally, they utilise various sensors to detect obstacles, ensuring safe flight paths.
